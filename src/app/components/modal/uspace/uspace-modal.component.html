U-Space

Overview

U-Space is a user interface to detect drones based off of hardware such as radars, Radio Frequencies, and PTZ cameras. Created for cpni as machine interface for detection hardware.
This project was created under the CPNI specifications. It was less focused on design and more focused on functionality, as it incorperated a lot of hardware.
CPNI stands for Centre for the Protection of National Infrastructure. They are the UK governmentâ€™s National Technical Authority for physical and personnel protective security.

My Role

My role for this project was to be the Front End developer working in a small team alongside a senior developer. Due to the small size of the team, i would sometimes be asked to do some tasks for the back end.
I would have to report back to the senior developer each time I completed a Jira ticket, he would then review my work and either tell me to fix something or merge it into the main branch There were no assigned designers so the design of the webapp was decided by the CEO or as a joint effort amongst the dev team.
