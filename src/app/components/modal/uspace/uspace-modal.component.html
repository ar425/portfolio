U-Space

Overview

U-Space is a user interface to detect drones based off of hardware such as radars, Radio Frequencies, and PTZ cameras.
This project was created under the CPNI specifications. It was less focused on design and more focused on functionality, as it incorperated a lot of hardware.
CPNI stands for Centre for the Protection of National Infrastructure. They are the UK governmentâ€™s National Technical Authority for physical and personnel protective security.
