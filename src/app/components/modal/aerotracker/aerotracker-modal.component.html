AeroTracker

Overview

AeroTracker is a live flight tracker webapp which tracks aircrafts such as airplanes, helicopters, and drones.
It allows a user to create zones - receive notifications if the zone detects anything - filter aircraft by altitude or speed, and other things relating to aircraft detection.
This interface integrates hardware such as radars and ADS-B, which is what allows it to display aircraft that this hardware is detecting.

Target Audience

The target audience for this project is anywhere from individual people wanting to protect their garden to enterprise busineses wanting to monitor and have protection from illegal drones. Government bodies have also shown high interest in this product.

Aims & Objectives

This product aims to provide individuals and bisnesses with full and comprehensive view of everything that flies, inorder to enable a safe sharing of our skies in the future. "With full situational awareness, the skies are unlocked"

My Role

"AeroTracker is the only flight tracking app in the world to fully integrate planes and drones on one interface, and give full situational awareness in real-time to all who need it." My role for this project was to be the Front End developer working in a small team alongside a senior developer and a backend developer. I would have to report back to the senior developer each time I completed a Jira ticket There were no assigned designers so the design of the webapp was decided by the CEO or as a joint effort amongst the dev team.
