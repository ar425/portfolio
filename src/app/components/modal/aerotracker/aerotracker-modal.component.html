AeroTracker

Overview

AeroTracker is a live flight tracker webapp which tracks aircrafts such as airplanes, helicopters, and drones. Increases airspace safety by improving awareness
It allows a user to create zones (customised geographical warning zones) - receive notifications if the zone detects anything - filter aircraft by altitude or speed, and other things relating to aircraft detection. When an aircraft is being detected inside a designated area, real-time alerts and notifications are displayed on the interface and can be sent to a device of the user's choice - including mobile phones and smart watches
This interface integrates hardware such as radars and ADS-B, which is what allows it to display aircraft that this hardware is detecting.

Target Audience

The target audience for this project is anywhere from individual people wanting to protect their garden to enterprise busineses wanting to monitor and have protection from illegal drones. Government bodies have also shown high interest in this product. For authorities and air traffic managers to identify and track drones alongside other forms of aviation in their are of responsibility Invaluable to a wide range of industries, including aviation, defence & security, insurance and more.

Aims & Objectives

This product aims to provide individuals and bisnesses with full and comprehensive view of everything that flies, inorder to enable a safe sharing of our skies in the future. "With full situational awareness, the skies are unlocked" "AeroTracker is the only flight tracking app in the world to fully integrate planes and drones on one interface, and give full situational awareness in real-time to all who need it."
